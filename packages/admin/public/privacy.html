Privacy Policy

Q Order Admin — A product of Fyn Arc Techworks

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Fyn Arc Techworks ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Q Order Admin mobile application ("App") available on Android and iOS. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When your restaurant account is created through the Q Order web application, the following information is collected:

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Full name

This information is entered on the web application and accessed by the mobile app to authenticate and identify you.

Restaurant Data

The App processes restaurant operational data including:

  • Orders placed by customers
  • Menu items and availability status
  • Table status and management data

Device Information

We collect minimal device information solely for crash reporting and app stability:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system and version

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect your location, contacts, camera data, photos, payment card details, or any financial information through this App.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide restaurant order management services through the App
  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • To send push notifications about new orders and order updates (with your permission)
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues using device information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Ever.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored on secure servers located in India (Hostinger VPS with HTTPS/TLS encryption).
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device only, using encrypted device storage. They are never transmitted to third parties.
  • Your data is retained for as long as your restaurant account remains active.
  • Upon account deletion, all associated personal data is removed from our servers within 30 days.

4. Your Rights under the DPDP Act 2023

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India), you have the following rights as a Data Principal:

  • Right to Access — Request a summary of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction — Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Erasure — Request deletion of your personal data, subject to any legal obligations we may have to retain certain records.
  • Right to Grievance Redressal — Raise concerns about how your data is handled.
  • Right to Nominate — Nominate another individual to exercise your rights in case of your incapacity or death.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at fynarctechworks@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Expo — Used as the app development framework. No user data is shared with Expo.

We do not integrate any analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or third-party tracking services in this App.

6. Children's Privacy

Q Order Admin is a business-to-business application designed exclusively for restaurant owners, managers, and staff. It is not directed at or intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child, we will promptly delete it.
