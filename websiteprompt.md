### 2.2 Features Page

Deep-dive into each feature with full-width sections alternating left/right mockups:

**Section 1: QR Code Ordering**
- Headline: "Your menu, on every phone — no download required"
- Body: Customers scan a table QR code → land on a beautiful, mobile-first menu → browse categories with icons, see item photos, read Veg/Non-Veg info → customise with modifiers (spice level, add-ons, special instructions) → add to cart → place order from their phone → track real-time status. No app store. No signup. No friction.
- Mockup: **iPhone 15 Pro frame** showing customer menu page with item detail drawer open
- Callout badges: "PWA — no install needed" · "Veg/Non-Veg filter" · "Real-time menu sync"

**Section 2: Live Order Dashboard**
- Headline: "Every order, the moment it's placed"
- Body: Orders flow in real-time onto your Kanban board — New → Confirmed → Preparing → Ready → Served. Drag and drop to update status. Click any order to see items, customisations, special instructions, and the full bill breakdown. Filter by status, search by table or order number, export to CSV or PDF.
- Mockup: **MacBook Pro frame** showing dashboard with Kanban board
- Callout: "Live updates — no page refresh" · "CSV & PDF export" · "Print bill"

**Section 3: Kitchen Display System (KDS)**
- Headline: "Your kitchen, always in sync"
- Body: A dedicated full-screen Kitchen Display System built for your kitchen staff. Two columns — Preparing and Ready to Serve. Each ticket shows the order number, table name, item names, quantities, customisations, and special instructions. A live prep timer changes colour — green under 10 minutes, amber at 10–15 minutes, red beyond 15. Audio alert on every new order. Mark items ready with one tap.
- Mockup: **MacBook Pro frame** showing KDS in full-screen mode with colour-coded timers
- Callout: "Colour-coded timers" · "Audio alerts" · "Full-screen mode"

**Section 4: Menu Management**
- Headline: "Your menu, your way — with full modifier support"
- Body: Create unlimited categories and menu items. Add photos, prices, and dietary tags (Veg / Non-Veg). Build modifier groups — size, add-ons, extras — with individual price adjustments. Toggle item availability instantly; the change reflects on every customer's phone in seconds. Bulk import and export your menu.
- Mockup: **MacBook Pro frame** showing admin menu page with item editor open
- Callout: "Instant sync to customer app" · "Bulk import/export" · "Modifier groups"

**Section 5: Smart Inventory**
- Headline: "Never run out of an ingredient mid-service"
- Body: Link ingredients to menu items with recipe quantities in kg, g, L, ml, or pieces. When an order is placed, stock is deducted automatically. Set minimum stock thresholds — Q Order alerts you when stock falls below the limit. If an ingredient hits zero, the menu item is automatically marked unavailable. Full stock movement history: Stock In, Order Deduct, Manual Adjustment, Wastage.
- Mockup: **MacBook Pro frame** showing inventory dashboard with stock levels and alerts
- Callout: "Auto-deduction per order" · "Zero-stock auto-disables item" · "Hourly low-stock alerts"

**Section 6: Staff & Attendance**
- Headline: "Your team, fully managed"
- Body: Add staff with role-based access — Owner, Admin, Manager, Staff. Each role sees only what they need. Track daily attendance — present, late, absent, on leave. Staff request leave; managers approve or deny from the panel. Payroll run tracking built in. Auto-lock the screen after idle time with a 6-digit PIN.
- Mockup: **MacBook Pro frame** showing staff management with attendance records
- Callout: "4 access roles" · "Daily attendance" · "Leave management" · "Auto-lock PIN"

**Section 7: Discounts & Coupons**
- Headline: "Run offers that actually fill tables"
- Body: Create percentage or flat-amount discounts. Set minimum order value, maximum discount cap, and schedule by date range or day of the week. Attach coupon codes with per-customer and total usage limits. Discounts can auto-apply or require a code at checkout. Track usage in real-time.
- Mockup: **MacBook Pro frame** showing discounts page with coupon creation form
- Callout: "Auto-apply or coupon code" · "Usage limit controls" · "Day-of-week scheduling"

**Section 8: Analytics & Reports**
- Headline: "Know your numbers — grow your business"
- Body: Revenue trends by day, week, month, or custom date range. Top-selling items and categories. Peak hours analysis. Payment method breakdown (cash, card, UPI). Table performance and turnover. Item-level sales and inventory consumption reports. Staff attendance reports. Export any report to Excel.
- Mockup: **MacBook Pro frame** showing analytics page with revenue charts
- Callout: "Excel export" · "Custom date ranges" · "10+ report types"

**Section 9: Thermal Printing**
- Headline: "KOT and bills — printed automatically"
- Body: Connect any ESC/POS compatible thermal printer over Bluetooth or your local network via TCP/IP. Auto-print Kitchen Order Tickets the moment an order is placed. Print detailed bills with your restaurant name, item breakdown, modifiers, GST, and payment summary. Configure printer connection and paper width from settings.
- Mockup: **MacBook Pro frame** showing printer settings configuration
- Callout: "Bluetooth & network printers" · "ESC/POS compatible" · "Auto-print on order"

**Section 10: Group Ordering**
- Headline: "One table, many phones — one order"
- Body: Create a group session with a shareable code. Friends at the same table join from their own phones and build their own cart individually. Everyone marks themselves ready. The host reviews the combined order and submits in one tap. All carts sync in real-time across every phone.
- Mockup: **iPhone 15 Pro frame** showing group dashboard with participant carts
- Callout: "Real-time sync" · "Per-person cart" · "Host submits all at once"

**Section 11: CRM — Customer Intelligence**
- Headline: "Know your regulars. Reward your VIPs."
- Body: Every customer who orders builds a profile automatically. View total visits, total spend, and average order value. Tag customers — VIP, Regular, New, Corporate, Inactive. Add private notes. Filter and search your full customer database. See full interaction history: every order, every feedback, every payment.
- Mockup: **MacBook Pro frame** showing CRM page with customer detail drawer open
- Callout: "Auto-built profiles" · "VIP tagging" · "Full order history"

**Section 12: Customer Feedback**
- Headline: "Turn every meal into a review"
- Body: Customers rate their experience across Food, Service, and Ambience. View aggregate scores, star distribution (1–5), and individual written feedback — all linked to the order and the customer. Understand what's working and what needs attention, without leaving your dashboard.
- Mockup: **MacBook Pro frame** showing feedback page with rating summary and review cards
- Callout: "Food · Service · Ambience ratings" · "Linked to orders" · "Star distribution chart"

**Section 13: QSR Counter Mode**
- Headline: "For the counter — quick service, zero friction"
- Body: A dedicated counter-side interface for takeaway and walk-in orders. Browse your full menu, add items, apply discounts, and place orders without a table. Hold tickets and recall them later for multi-queue counters. Print the KOT per kitchen station and the final bill at checkout.
- Mockup: **MacBook Pro frame** showing QSR page with menu grid and cart
- Callout: "Hold & recall tickets" · "Takeaway orders" · "Per-station KOT"

## 3. Design Guidelines

### Layout
- Full-width sections, max-width `1280px` content area
- Alternating `#FFFFFF` and `#FFF7ED` section backgrounds
- 80px+ whitespace between sections
- 8px grid system

### Typography (Quicksand only)
- **Font**: `Quicksand` for ALL text — no secondary font
- **Hero headings**: 700 weight, 48–64px, `#1F2937`
- **Section headings**: 600 weight, 36–48px
- **Sub-headings**: 500 weight, 24–32px
- **Body**: 400 weight, 16–18px, line-height 1.75, `#4B5563`
- **Muted/caption**: 400 weight, 14px, `#6B7280`
- **CTAs**: 600 weight, 16px
- **Fallback**: `system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", Roboto, sans-serif`

### Color Rules
- **Primary actions**: `#F97316`
- **Primary hover**: `#EA6C0A`
- **Accent** (badges, icons, decorative): `#FDBA74`
- **Page bg**: alternating `#FFFFFF` / `#FFF7ED`
- **Cards**: `#FFFFFF` with `shadow-card`
- **Glow on hover**: `0 0 20px rgba(249, 115, 22, 0.25)`
- **Badge bg**: `rgba(249, 115, 22, 0.1)`

### Buttons
- Primary: `bg-[#F97316]` text-white, rounded-xl, px-8 py-4, hover `bg-[#EA6C0A]`, 200ms
- Secondary: `border-[#F97316] text-[#F97316]`, rounded-xl, hover `bg-[rgba(249,115,22,0.1)]`
- Accent pill: `bg-[#FED7AA] text-[#F97316]`, rounded-full

### Mockup Guidelines
- **Customer app** → **iPhone 15 Pro** frame (Dynamic Island, titanium)
- **Admin panel** → **MacBook Pro** frame or browser window
- Use REAL product screenshots only — no placeholder UI
- iPhone: slight 3D tilt (5–10°) + drop shadow
- MacBook: flat or slight upward tilt
- Hero: iPhone left, MacBook right (slightly overlapping)
- Feature sections: alternate left/right across sections

### Animations — SaaS-Grade Motion

**Principles:**
- Easing: `cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)` for all entrances
- Duration: 600–800ms reveals, 200–300ms micro-interactions
- Stagger: 100–150ms between siblings
- Always respect `prefers-reduced-motion`

**Hero:**
- Headline: line-by-line `clipPath` reveal, 800ms stagger
- Subheadline: fade-up 200ms after headline
- CTAs: scale 0.95→1 + fade, 100ms stagger
- iPhone: float-in left, `rotateY(8deg)`→`0`, 1000ms
- MacBook: float-in right, mirror
- Trust badges: fade-up, 100ms stagger, 500ms delay
- Background: animated gradient with `#FDBA74` at 5% opacity

**How It Works:**
- Progress line draws between steps 1→2→3 on scroll
- Cards: scale 0.9→1 + fade on scroll intersection
- Step numbers: `#FDBA74` circular progress ring

**Feature cards:**
- `translateY(40px)`→`0`, 100ms stagger
- Hover: lift 4px + `shadow-elevated` + orange border glow
- Icon: slow pulse (1→1.05→1, 3s loop)

**Feature sections:**
- Parallax: mockup at 0.8× scroll speed
- Text: `translateY(30px)`→`0`, 600ms
- Mockup: slide-in from side + scale 0.95→1, 800ms
- Callout badges: spring pop-in, 150ms stagger

**Comparison table:**
- Rows: 50ms stagger on scroll
- ✅: spring scale-in (1→1.15→1)
- ❌: fade with subtle red flash
- Header: sticky + backdrop blur

**Pricing cards:**
- 3D cursor tilt with `perspective(1000px)`
- "Popular" badge: bounce loop (0→-4px→0, 2s)
- Price: count-up 0→actual on scroll
- Recommended plan CTA: glow pulse loop

**Stats:**
- Count-up 0→target, 2s easeOut, on scroll
- Decorative `#FDBA74` dots, 10% opacity, slow drift

**Scroll progress bar:** 3px `#F97316` at top of page

**Mobile:** replace parallax with fade-in, reduce durations 30%, swipe carousel

### Mobile
- Mobile-first
- Hamburger menu with slide-in drawer
- Stacked layouts (mockup top, text bottom)
- Full-width CTAs
- 48px min tap targets
- MacBook frames → browser-frame only on mobile
